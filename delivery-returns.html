Delivery and Returns Policy

Welcome to Triyet Wholesale! We're committed to providing you with high-quality wholesale products and exceptional service. Please take a moment to review our delivery and returns policy to understand how we handle the shipment of your orders and the process for returns or exchanges.

Delivery Information

Shipping Process

  • Order Processing: Once you place an order, our team will process it promptly. This includes verifying product availability, preparing your items, and scheduling delivery.
  • Shipping Method: We use reliable carriers to ensure the safe and timely delivery of your products. The shipping method may vary based on the size, weight, and destination of your order.
  • Delivery Time: Delivery times may vary depending on your location and the shipping method chosen. You will receive an estimated delivery date at the time of purchase. In case of any delays, we will inform you promptly.

Shipping Fees

Shipping fees are calculated based on the size and weight of your order, as well as the shipping destination. These fees will be clearly communicated to you during the checkout process.

Returns and Exchanges

We understand that there may be occasions when you need to return or exchange a product. Please review our policy on returns and exchanges:

Returns and Exchanges

How to Initiate a Return/Exchange

  • Contact our customer support team at [Customer Support Email/Phone] to request a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number.
  • Clearly label the package with the RMA number and return it to the provided address.
  • Once we receive the returned product, our team will inspect it to ensure it meets the eligibility criteria.

Eligibility for Returns/Exchanges

Returns or exchanges are accepted within 28 days from the date of delivery but products must be in their original, unused, and undamaged condition.

Refund or Exchange

For approved returns, we will process a refund to your original payment method, or you can opt for an exchange based on product availability. Please allow 3-7 business days for the processing of returns or exchanges.
